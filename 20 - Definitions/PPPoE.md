#### PPPoE
- *Point-to-Point Protocol over Ethernet (PPPoE)* is a widely used protocol by ISPs to provision DSL high-speed internet services
- Encapsulates [[PPP]] frames inside [[802.3 Frames|Ethernet frames]]
