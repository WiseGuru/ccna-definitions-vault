#### Gratuitous ARP
- *Gratuitous ARP* is an [[ARP|ARP Response]] that was not sent in response to an [[ARP|ARP Request]]
	- It is sent [[Broadcast Address|Broadcast]] as a way to announce an update to its [[IPv4|IP address]] or [[MAC Address]]
