#### Half-duplex
- Nodes on a *half-duplex* link are on the same [[Collision Domain]], and cannot send and receive data at the same time.
	- Notably, [[Hub|hubs]] and [[Wireless Access Points|Wireless APs]] function on *half-duplex* networks
