#### Transmission Speed
- The *speed* of an interface is the *rate* at which it can *send and receive data*
	- e.g., 100Mbps, 1Gbps, etc.
- Normally, interfaces *auto-negotiate* their speed transmission rate, defaulting to the lowest rate possible
