#### Leased Lines
- Leased lines are *dedicated physical links* that use [[Serial]] connections (with [[PPP]] or [[HDLC]] encapsulation)
- Leased lines are falling out of fashion, and being replaced by Ethernet WAN
	- More expensive
	- Longer installation time
	- Slower speeds
