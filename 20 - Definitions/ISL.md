#### ISL
- *Cisco Inter-Switch Link (ISL)* is a #Cisco-Proprietary protocol that conveys [[VLAN]] information in traffic between switches and routers.
	- Now deprecated, in favor of the [[dot1q|802.1Q]] standard.
