#### Frequency
- The number of cycles/oscillations for a unit of time
	- The reciprocal of a [[Period]], 

#### Hertz
- *Hertz* is the number of cycles in a second
	- kHz = 1,000 cycles per second
	- GHz = 1,000,000,000 cycles per second
