#### Segment
- A *Segment* is the data unit for [[Layer 4|Layer 4]] of the OSI model
	- If the transport protocol is [[TCP]], the data unit is called a *Segment*
	- If the transport protocol is [[UDP]], then it's called a *Datagram*
